# ApplyOps Buyer Intake

Send this after purchase.

## Required

1. Current resume.
2. LinkedIn URL.
3. Target role titles.
4. Target locations or remote preference.
5. Work authorization constraints.
6. Compensation constraints.
7. Earliest start date.
8. 3 to 5 job postings they want to target.
9. Any employers, degrees, certifications, metrics, or titles they do not want used unless explicitly verified.

## Boundaries

We do not fabricate credentials, employers, dates, degrees, titles, or metrics.

We do not submit applications without user approval.

We do not store high-risk PII such as SSN, DOB, or passport/ID numbers.

We do not promise interviews or job offers.
