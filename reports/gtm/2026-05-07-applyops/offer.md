## Positioning

ApplyOps is a human-in-the-loop job application operating system for serious job seekers who are getting rejected, ghosted, or buried by generic AI resumes.

The offer is not a blind auto-apply bot. The deliverable is a truth-locked job search sprint:

- Resume truth audit against candidate-provided source facts.
- ATS-safe resume cleanup and targeting notes.
- Job-fit scoring and priority queue.
- Referral/outreach drafts for high-value roles.
- Product proposal outline for Tier 1 roles.
- Application tracker review with evidence requirements.
- 7-day execution plan for better applications.
