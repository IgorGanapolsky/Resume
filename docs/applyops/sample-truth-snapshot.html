Sample Resume Truth Snapshot

This is a synthetic example showing what the $9 snapshot flags: risky claims, readability issues, and one practical next action.

Resume Excerpt

Senior Software Engineer

  • Led AI platform modernization that saved the company millions.
  • Owned production Kubernetes, security, data pipelines, and all executive reporting.
  • Increased application conversion by 85% using machine learning.

Snapshot Findings

Risk 1: Unsupported money claim

"Saved the company millions" is high-risk unless the candidate can show the model, baseline, and finance source.

Led modernization work on the AI platform, including reliability fixes, deployment cleanup, and operating dashboards used by engineering stakeholders.

Risk 2: Overbroad ownership claim

"Owned production Kubernetes, security, data pipelines, and all executive reporting" sounds inflated unless one person truly owned all of those areas.

Supported production Kubernetes services and partnered with security, data, and reporting teams on release readiness and operational reviews.

Risk 3: Unverified conversion metric

"Increased application conversion by 85%" needs a measurement window, funnel definition, and analytics source. Without that, it reads like AI filler.

Improved application funnel instrumentation and identified drop-off points that informed follow-up product changes.

ATS / Readability Issue

The bullets are too broad for role matching. They mention AI, Kubernetes, security, data, executive reporting, and conversion without showing the specific job family.

Next Action

Pick one target lane before applying again, then rewrite the top third of the resume around that lane using only claims the candidate can defend in an interview.
